## Contribute to our soluitons

### Report user needs for new or changed features

If you miss features in the solution, or you think that something should be changed to cover needs better, you are welcome to comment on needs that has been reported by others, or report new needs.

[Overview over reported user needs](https://github.com/orgs/Informasjonsforvaltning/projects/12)

New user needs may be reported in [the Behov-repo i Github](https://github.com/Informasjonsforvaltning/behov/issues)

You may also [contact us](/contact) to report user needs.

### Report bugs

Bus may be reported in [Fdk issue tracker (Github)](https://github.com/Informasjonsforvaltning/fdk-issue-tracker/issues) Choose 'New Issue' and 'Bug Rapport' to open the correct template for reporting bugs.

You may also [contact us](/contact) to report bugs.

### Contribute with code

It is possible to give code contributions directly to our services, in case you wish to contribute to solve a user need or bug.

Please [contact us](/contact) when you deliever a contribution, to prepare us on the incoming contribution and make us better equipped to handle it as quickly as possible.
