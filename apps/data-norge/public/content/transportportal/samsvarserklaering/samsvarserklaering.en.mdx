# Declaration of conformity

Data owners and service providers who share their data and services in the portal must comply with the requirements of the EU regulations regarding declarations of conformity. For Regulation 886/2013, data providers of traffic-safety-related information submit their declaration of conformity to Vegtilsynet. For Regulation 962/2015 and 1926/2017, the clarification of practice is ongoing. More information will follow.
