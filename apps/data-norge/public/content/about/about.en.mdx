## What is Data.norge.no?

### Purpose

Data.norge.no provides an overview of what data the public sector has and can share. The catalog facilitates providing users with easier access to this type of data. The catalog also promotes fair terms of competition by enabling the reuse of data for both commercial and non-commercial purposes.

### What does Data.norge.no have to offer?

Data.norge.no provides a comprehensive overview of datasets, concepts, APIs, information models, services, and events in the public sector, including protected data. Here, you will get help to discover, evaluate, and use data with clear information on data providers, availability and data quality, among other things. You may find information on whether the data are open, or if they have restricted access, as well as how the data is related to each other, and what they mean.

The content on Data.norge.no is provided the organizations themselves. Each organization is responsible for managing their content in he catalogs. The Norwegian Digitalisation Agency is responsible for the operation and development of the website.

## Search

The main feature of Data.norge.no is the search, which allows you to find the data or information you're looking for. You can use the Artificial Intelligence-driven search or standard text search with advanced filters.
