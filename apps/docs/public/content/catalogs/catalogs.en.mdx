# Our Data Catalogs

<Ingress>
    Data.norge.no provides a comprehensive overview of datasets, concepts, APIs, information models, services and
    events, and artificial intelligence projects in the public sector.
</Ingress>

Here you can find help to discover, evaluate, and use data, with clear information about availability, data owners, and usage possibilities. You will learn whether the data is publicly available or requires specific permissions, as well as how the data is related and what it means.

The content on Data.norge.no is provided by various public and private organizations. The Norwegian Digitalisation Agency is responsible for the operation and development of the website.
